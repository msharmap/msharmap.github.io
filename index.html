Sharma Research Group

The Sharma Research Group focuses on development of computational methods to study both fluid and material systems. Our current focus is on developing machine learning models for materials discovery, uncertainty quantification of chemically reacting flows in hypersonic regimes and studying materials for energy harvesting and sustainable aviation. We use a combination of quantum chemistry calcutions, molecular dynamics simulations and computational fluid dynamics to study these systems. Find out more about our research here.
